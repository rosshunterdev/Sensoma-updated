Each session is designed specifically for you — guided from consultation and with full agreement. My journey into being an energetic healer began with my maternal grandmother, a gifted energy reader, medium and intuitive. Her wisdom taught me to respect, sense, feel, and work with energy in a way that brings real insight and change.

With a scientific background I struggled to align the physical and energetic body — but over time I accepted the reality that both are essential parts of our self/being. With focus I developed a strong intuitive practice: identifying patterns, blockages, and imbalances within the energetic field.
