At Sensoma, we believe in creating a safe, restorative space where body, mind, and spirit can reconnect. Our treatments combine therapeutic techniques with intuitive healing to restore balance and resilience

Initial Consultation

(as part of the first treatment)

To offer a tailored treatment a brief health history will be completed, this will take approx 5-10 minutes or can be completed online prior to the treatment, and include current symptoms and hopes or expectations for the session.

What does a treatment involve?

Each session is designed specifically for you. Guided from the initial consultation and with full agreement, the session will be created to best reflect your needs. This may involve a focused treatment for an injury or specific area of pain or encompass a combination of neuro-muscular, trigger point therapy or intuitive healing depending on preferences and needs. Treatments are restorative and preventative and can build strength and resilience when completed as a series of sessions.
